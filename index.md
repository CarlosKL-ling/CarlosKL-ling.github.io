# Carlos K.L.
### PhD Candidate in Hispanic Linguistics
**Specializing in Morphosyntax & Experimental Syntax**

Welcome to my academic website! I am finishing my PhD at the University of Arizona, where my research bridges the gap between formal theoretical frameworks and experimental processing data.

## Research Interests
* **Theoretical:** Morphosyntax, Minimalism, Distributed Morphology (DM), Spanish clitics in periphrases, copular constructions.
* **Experimental:** Syntactic processing, Self-paced reading, and Pupillometry.

## Education

* MA in Linguistics, Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú
